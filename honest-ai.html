Honest AI. Our favorite sales pitch.

No black-box forecasts. No autonomous agents inside your systems. The math is published.

AI models are not reliable reasoners. They are excellent accelerators and researchers. No model ever computes your money. AI reads and reconciles messy sources, proposes scenarios and rules for human review, and narrates results after the numbers are final.
