03 · ROI model

A transparent model, not a black box.

Commercial value reviewed=

Relevant sales exposure
× expected decision impact
× confidence adjustment

Margin protected=

Commercial value reviewed
× gross margin rate
× estimated avoidable loss

Decision-cycle improvement=

Baseline decision time
− Outturn-assisted decision time

Knowledge reuse rate=

Decisions using relevant prior decisions, documents or expertise
÷ total decisions prepared

Outcome connection rate=

Completed decisions linked to a measurable result
÷ total completed decisions

Every number should be recomputable from the retailer’s own source data and decision history. Do not claim value that cannot be traced back to the underlying assumptions.
