Where we sit

The decision layer between commercial intelligence and execution.

Outturn works with the systems retail teams already use. It brings their context together around the commercial decision, preserves the judgment applied and connects it to the outcome.

CategoryWhat the system doesWhat Outturn adds
Planning and forecastingModels what should happen.Prepares the decision when new commercial context changes the plan.
BI and reportingShows what happened.Connects the context, judgment and outcome behind the decision.
Operational systemsRecords the approved action.Preserves why it was chosen, the trade-offs and what happened next.
Generic AI assistantsSummarize available information.Works from retail context, prior decisions and relevant human expertise.

Outturn does not replace planning, allocation or operational systems. It adds the missing decision layer above them.
